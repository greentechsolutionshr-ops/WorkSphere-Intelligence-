The OHI Framework

Five pillars.
One whole-system score.

The Organisational Health Index is WorkSphere Intelligence's proprietary diagnostic framework. It measures the five interconnected conditions that determine whether your people — and therefore your organisation — can truly flourish.

Each pillar is scored 0–100. The composite OHI Score tells you where you stand. The dimension-level breakdown tells you exactly why — and where to act first.

How It Works

From survey to strategic clarity
in four steps.

The OHI process is designed to be light on your organisation and heavy on insight. We handle everything — from survey deployment to report delivery — so your people experience is smooth and your results are actionable.

01
Diagnostic Survey
We deploy the 35-question OHI survey across your organisation. Fully anonymous. Mixed format. Estimated 12–15 minutes to complete. We handle all communications and technical delivery.
1–2 weeks
02
Analysis & Scoring
Your responses are scored across all five pillars using our weighted methodology. We analyse quantitative scores, qualitative themes from open-text responses, and identify significant cohort-level patterns.
5–10 business days
03
OHI Report
You receive a comprehensive, branded report — your OHI Score, pillar breakdowns, risk flags, performance impact statements, anonymised verbatim insight, and a prioritised 90-day action matrix.
Delivered to you
04
Debrief & Action
We present and discuss the findings with your leadership team. Optional: a Leadership Debrief Workshop and Action Planning Session to turn insight into a concrete implementation plan with clear ownership.

WorkSphere Intelligence was built on a simple but stubborn belief: that the most important work any organisation can do is create the conditions for its people to flourish — and that this work is too often neglected, unmeasured, and undervalued.

The Organisational Health Index exists because engagement surveys aren't enough. They tell you how people feel today. The OHI tells you why — and what to do about it. It measures the whole system: culture, safety, inclusion, wellbeing, and leadership, all in one integrated diagnostic.

WorkSphere Intelligence is part of a broader ecosystem of people solutions, operating alongside GTSC (HR Consulting & Staffing) and EliteConnectBPO (Operations & Business Process Outsourcing) — so that when the OHI identifies a need, the right expertise is always within reach.
